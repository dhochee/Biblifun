Welcome to Biblifun!

The purpose of this website is to help improve your memory of Bible scriptures, and have fun at the same time!

You can practice on your own, or play collaborative games with friends where you work together or form teams. The goal is not to compete, but to learn.

What Can You Do Here?
  • Practice scripture memorization using flash cards.
  • Create your own scipture sets for practice, including your own summaries.
  • Play games with friends, like Jeopardy.
